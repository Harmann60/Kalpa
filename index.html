Getting to Kalpa.

The journey to 2,960m is populated with scenic landscapes, making the trip an affair you will always remember.

By Air

The nearest airport is at Shimla, 267 km from Kalpa. It connects to major cities like Delhi and Mumbai. Cabs and taxis are easily available outside the airport directly to Kalpa.

By Train

The nearest railway station is Shimla (260 km), a major hub connecting to cities including Delhi. From there, you can easily hire a taxi or take a scenic bus ride to Kalpa.

By Road

The most convenient option. HRTC offers regular bus services from Shimla, Delhi, and Punjab. Private buses from Delhi are also available.

Popular Routes:

  • 1. Delhi → Shimla → Rampur → Kalpa
  • 2. Delhi → Manali → Kaza → Kalpa

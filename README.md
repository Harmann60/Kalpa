# MyKalpa 🏔️ 
**A Himalayan Paradise**

A premium, minimalist tourism website dedicated to Kalpa, Himachal Pradesh. Inspired by the clean, immersive design language of high-end European tourism platforms (like MySwitzerland), this project showcases the beauty of the Kinner Kailash region through high-quality visuals, functional "Swiss-style" UI/UX, and lightning-fast performance.

## 🏔️ About Kalpa (The Inspiration)
Nestled at an altitude of 2,960 meters (9,711 ft) in the Kinnaur district of Himachal Pradesh, India, Kalpa is a place of raw, untouched majesty. Overlooking the Sutlej River valley, it is famous for its dramatic landscapes that rival the Swiss Alps, earning it the title "The Switzerland of India."

I built this project to capture the essence of Kalpa, highlighting its unique characteristics:
* **The Sacred Peaks:** The majestic Kinner Kailash range, featuring the legendary 79-foot high rock pillar (Shivling) that magically changes colors as the sun moves across the sky.
* **Cultural Harmony:** A beautiful, seamless blend of Hinduism and Buddhism, deeply visible in the wooden architecture of the Narayan-Nagini Temple and the ancient Hu-Bu-Lan-Kar Monastery.
* **The Landscape:** Endless stretches of lush apple orchards, dense pine and deodar forests, and thrilling geographic wonders like the vertical drops of "Suicide Point."
* **The Journey:** Accessed via the legendary Hindustan-Tibet Road, offering one of the most thrilling and scenic road trips in the world.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Immersive Video Hero:** A full-screen, autoplaying background video showcasing the majesty of Kalpa, complete with a dark gradient overlay for typography readability.
* **Interactive Booking Widget:** A dark-themed, tabbed interface built with Vanilla JS that allows users to seamlessly search for Flights, Trains, and HRTC Buses, redirecting them to official booking portals.
* **Asymmetric Destinations Grid:** A modern, CSS-grid layout featuring top spots like the Kinner Kailash Range, Suicide Point, and Roghi Village. Includes smooth, slide-up hover animations for destination details.
* **Responsive Planning Guide:** A clean, 3-column "How to Reach" layout utilizing custom SVG icons and structured typography, ensuring a flawless experience on desktop and mobile.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
This project was intentionally built without heavy frameworks to ensure maximum performance and instantaneous load times.
* **HTML5:** Semantic, accessible structure.
* **Tailwind CSS:** Utility-first styling via CDN to achieve a minimalist, premium look rapidly.
* **Vanilla JavaScript:** For lightweight interactivity (tab switching and booking portal redirection).
